About Us

A4ALGO is a club under Abacus Society managed by the students of GLA University to build a strong base of programming in every student through various events and workshops.

Aim and Objectives

  • Developing algorithmic and technical skills.

  • Focusing on understanding the basics of programming languages.

  • Build strong coders through various competitions.

  • Developing competitive spirit among the students.

Scope

  • Through the activities of club students can enhance their coding skills.

  • Students will be able to solve any problem logically and coding will become easy.

  • Workshops will provide them knowledge and help them know their interests better.
