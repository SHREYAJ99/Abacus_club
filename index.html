About Our University

GLA University is situated in Mathura in Uttar Pradesh state of India. Established in 1998,it is accredited from UGC,AICTE,NAAC,PCI. GlA University offers 86 courses accross 12 streams namely Pharmacy, Commerce an Banking, Management, Science, Medical, Information Technology,Engineering, Law and accross 19 degrees like B.Tech, BBA, BSc.,B.Com etc.

This University has a policy to proovide an empowering learning experience in established and emerging professions and capacity-building for leadership, enterpreneurship and innovation.
