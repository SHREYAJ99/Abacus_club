Aim and Objectives

The objective of the club is to organize workshops, events, and seminars for the students to make them aware of the fascinating career in the field of Data Analytics and Data Science.

  • We aim at providing guidance, study path, and practical knowledge to members to inculcate interest in building socio-economic models, health models, etc. to help the society.

  • We aim at whetting your existing skills and gaining hands-on experience as well as research experience in Data Mining, Data Wrangling, Data Visualization, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Artificial Intelligence, etc.
